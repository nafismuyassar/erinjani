DISCOVER MOUNT RINJANI WITH 2 DAYS / 1 NIGHT TREK TO THE SUMMIT (3,726M)

ADVANCE LEVEL

Perfect for those seeking a more challenging adventure, this route features long, open grassland trails with little shade and intense sun exposure. The hike demands strong physical endurance, especially on summit day, where trekkers face a steep, sandy path each step forward often followed by a half-step slide back. Despite the cardiovascular and tough terrain, reaching the summit rewards you with breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Rinjani, Lombok Island, and surrounding areas.

NOTE: We strongly recommend doing regular exercise, such as jogging or running, 2-3 times per week for at least 3 weeks before your trek. A good level of physical fitness is required.

PREPARATION DAY

  • Trek briefing at your hotel
  • Finalize preparation for the hike
  • Meet your guide

DAY 1: TREKKING TO SEMBALUN CRATER RIM

  • 06:30 AM: Breakfast at the hotel
  • 07:00 AM: Pick-up by our driver. Drive to the starting point with your guide and porters (approx. 1 hour)
  • 08:00 AM: Register at the Mount Rinjani National Park office
  • 08:40 AM: Start hiking through the beautiful savanna landscape
  • 11:30 AM: Arrive at Pos 2 (Shelter 2). Enjoy lunch prepared by your guide and porters.
  • 12:40 PM: Continue the trek to Pos 3 (approx. 1.5 hours)
  • 02:40 PM: Begin the steep ascent to Sembalun Crater Rim (approx. 3 hours)
  • 05:40 PM: Arrive at the campsite. Enjoy sunset views, followed by dinner.

DAY 2: SUMMIT ATTEMPT AND DESCENT

  • 01:30 AM: Wake up and have a light breakfast (toast and hot drinks)
  • 02:00 AM: Begin the summit hike (approx. 4 hours through volcanic sand and rocks)
  • 06:00 AM: Reach the summit. Enjoy the sunrise and panoramic views.
  • 07:00 AM: Descend back to the campsite (approx. 2 hours)
  • 09:00 AM: Have breakfast, pack your belongings
  • 10:00 AM: Begin the descent to Sembalun Village (approx. 5.5 hours)
  • Lunch break on the way down at Pos 2 or Pos 1.
  • Upon arrival in Sembalun Village, our driver will take you back to our office.
